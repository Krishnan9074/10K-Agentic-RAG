**Introduction to RAG Technology**

RAG (**Retrieval-Augmented Generation**) is a technique that combines information retrieval with large language models.
Its core idea is to first retrieve relevant content from an external knowledge base before generating a response, and then provide the retrieved results to the model as context.

A typical RAG system usually includes the following steps:

1. **Document Chunking**
2. **Embedding (Vectorization)**
3. **Similarity Retrieval**
4. **Answer Generation**

RAG can significantly reduce the probability of large language models generating incorrect or fabricated information (“hallucinations”).
