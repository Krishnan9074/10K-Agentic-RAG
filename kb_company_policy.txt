Company Internal Policy Summary

The company operates a flexible work schedule. Employees may arrange their 8 working hours between 9:00 AM and 10:00 PM.
All employees must comply with data security and privacy protection policies, and must not disclose internal data to third parties.

Leave Policy:
- Annual leave: 10 days per year
- Sick leave: A doctor's certificate is required
- Personal leave: Must be applied for in advance and approved

The final right of interpretation of this policy belongs to the company's Human Resources Department.
